01 — the place

a brownstone you can sit in all day.

front porch in the morning, a long alleyway through the middle, a quiet backyard for the afternoon. wifi, outlets, and a chocolate-chip scone if you got here early enough.

it was a parlor once, with the bones to prove it — pressed tin overhead, original molding around the windows, the slow give of an old wood floor underfoot. we kept what was kind and softened what wasn't. the espresso machine sits where the piano used to.

02 — what we do

two shifts, one room.

i.

Daytime cafe

espresso, drip, matcha, teas, and house pastries from 8am. counter service, long tables, a porch for the spring.

ii.

Evening wine bar

natural wines by the glass and bottle, wednesday through sunday. small plates from the kitchen, low light, the back garden when the weather allows.

iii.

The kitchen

a small kitchen pop-up rotates through the back of the room — snacks for wine service, plates for dinner, and an occasional weekend pastry.
